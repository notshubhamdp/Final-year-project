# Quick Start: Landlord Property Map Feature

## What's New?
Landlords can now easily locate their properties on an interactive map and save exact coordinates to help tenants find them.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Go to Your Property
1. Log in as a Landlord
2. Click "My Properties" in your dashboard
3. Select the property you want to locate

### Step 2: Access the Map
- Click the **"📍 View on Map"** button in the Property Location section

### Step 3: Set Location (Choose One Method)

#### Method A: Click on Map
1. The interactive map appears
2. **Click anywhere** on the map where your property is located
3. A marker will appear automatically
4. Coordinates update instantly

#### Method B: Search by Address
1. Enter your property address in the **"Search Address"** box
2. Press **Enter** or wait for suggestions
3. Map centers on the location
4. Marker is placed automatically

#### Method C: Manual Coordinates
1. If you know the exact coordinates:
   - Enter **Latitude** (e.g., 28.6139)
   - Enter **Longitude** (e.g., 77.2090)
2. The map will automatically update

### Step 4: Fine-Tune (Optional)
- **Drag the marker** on the map to adjust the exact location
- Coordinates update in real-time as you drag

### Step 5: Save
1. Review the coordinates displayed
2. Click **"💾 Save Location"** button
3. Your property location is saved!

## Tips & Tricks

✅ **Zoom In/Out**: Use scroll wheel or + / - buttons on map  
✅ **Pan Around**: Click and drag the map background  
✅ **Precise Placement**: Zoom in before placing marker  
✅ **Search Tips**: Try nearby landmarks if address doesn't work  
✅ **Common Coordinates**:
- New Delhi: 28.6139°N, 77.2090°E
- Mumbai: 19.0760°N, 72.8777°E
- Bangalore: 12.9716°N, 77.5946°E

## Coordinate Format

**Latitude** (North/South)
- Range: -90 to +90
- Positive = North of Equator
- Negative = South of Equator

**Longitude** (East/West)
- Range: -180 to +180
- Positive = East of Prime Meridian
- Negative = West of Prime Meridian

## What Happens After Saving?

1. ✅ Location is saved to your property details
2. ✅ Tenants can see your property location on the platform
3. ✅ Map displays on your property details page
4. ✅ You can edit it anytime by clicking "View on Map" again

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Map won't load | Check your internet connection |
| Can't find address | Try searching for nearby landmark or use coordinates |
| Marker in wrong place | Zoom in and drag marker to exact location, then save |
| Coordinates not saving | Make sure latitude is -90 to 90, longitude is -180 to 180 |
| Map too zoomed out | Click zoom in button or scroll to zoom closer |
