Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us at NIVASA

1. Introduction

NIVASA (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Smart Rent House Finder platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email address, phone number
  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Property listings and rental activity data
  • Device and usage information for analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To connect tenants and landlords
  • To process payments securely
  • To improve platform performance and user experience
  • To provide customer support

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Landlords or tenants for rental purposes
  • Payment gateways for secure transactions
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies & Tracking

NIVASA may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze usage, and improve user experience.

7. User Rights

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt out of promotional communications

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of NIVASA indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@nivasa.app
