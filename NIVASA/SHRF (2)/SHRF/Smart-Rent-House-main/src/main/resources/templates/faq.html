Frequently Asked Questions

Everything tenants and landlords need to know

Is NIVASA free for tenants ▼

Yes. Browsing properties, contacting landlords, and creating an account on NIVASA is completely free for tenants.

Are the properties on NIVASA verified ▼

Yes. All landlords and property listings go through a verification process to ensure safety and authenticity.

Can I directly contact the landlord ▼

Absolutely. NIVASA allows direct communication between tenants and landlords through secure contact details.

Does NIVASA charge brokerage fees ▼

No. NIVASA does not charge brokerage fees unless explicitly mentioned for premium services.

Can I save properties for later ▼

Yes. You can save favorite properties in your account and access them anytime.

Is listing a property free for landlords ▼

Yes. Basic property listing on NIVASA is free. Optional premium visibility may be introduced later.

How do I verify my property ▼

You will be asked to upload property details and documents, which are reviewed by our verification team.

How can I find genuine tenants ▼

NIVASA connects you with verified tenant profiles, reducing risk and improving trust.

Can I manage multiple properties ▼

Yes. Landlords can list and manage multiple properties from a single dashboard.

Does NIVASA handle rental agreements ▼

NIVASA assists with documentation and verification, but final agreements are between tenant and landlord.
